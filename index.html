About me

I am a high school student, I have done research in science and mathematics. Currently I am developing an extremely simple calculator that uses Tau instead of Pi as its default constant. I can be contacted via Twitter @Metaslaya

About my projects: The Pi-less Calculator

The Pi-less Calculator comes from the initiative of having a calculator that uses Tau as a constant instead of what almost every calculator uses, Pi. The project is not limited just to the creation of the calculator, the project seeks to stablish a new system to define the measure of an angle. Today we use radians (International System of Units) to measure angles, but if we could use another simpler system the teaching and understanding of the measure of angles would be easier.
