tauproject

The Pi-less Calculator comes from the initiative of having a calculator that uses Tau as a constant instead of what almost every calculator uses, Pi. The tauproject is not limited just to the creation of the calculator, the project seeks to establish a new system to obtain the measure of angles. Today we use radians (International System of Units) to measure angles, but if we could use another system the teaching and understanding of measuring angles would be more elementary.

How is the calculator being developed?

The developing of the calculator itself is being done with the help of JavaScript and its functions. The buttons and graphical units that make the usage of the calculator possible are being developed in HTML5 and CSS3. The intention is to make a calculator that can handle calculations in the most accurate, yet uncomplicated possible way.
